I am a 5th year PhD Candidate at Stanford Linguistics and Stanford NLP, advised by Dan Jurafsky and Beth Levin.
I am broadly interested in studying the language people use to communicate, especially in charged contexts, in order to better understand human minds and society.

I am currently based in the Bay Area, having previously lived in Ithaca, NY, Princeton, NJ, and Luoyang, China.
